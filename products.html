Our Services

Freight Forwarding

CargoTech Solutions provides full range of freight forwarding services including air, sea and land transports. They send your shipments on time, and within budget with our reliable carriers and global network of partners (Vlachos 2020). From pick-up to delivery, our entire logistics process is all managed thus you can experience better and worry-less service.

Real-Time Tracking

Logistics is all about transparency, and we enable this via our best-in-class real-time tracking system which you see to the whereabouts of your shipments. With our user-friendly tracking system, you can track your cargo status as well the current location and expected arrival time of delivery which ultimately brings relief and makes a sensible decision for yourself (Wang et al., 2019).

Warehousing and Distribution

We have secured, well-located and up-to-date warehousing facilities to achieve the most efficient distribution costs possible. We have a selection of available storage options, from temperature-sensitive facilities to mechanized stock list handling framework (Ross 2019). We create a distribution network that quickly gets your products to the people you serve, slashing lead times and improving end-to-end supply chain performance.

Customs Clearance

CargoTech Solutions can make international shipping easier. Our experienced team of custom clearance experts take care customs regulations, documentation and compliances to avoid any kind hitch in transit (Luo et al., 2021). We are always updated with the recent law regulations and work closely with customs authorities world-wide to speed up clearing process.

E-commerce Solutions

In the world of e-commerce, where everything is needed to be done quickly and accurately, CargoTech Solutions offers a holistic range of services in the e-commerce logistics space – from order fulfillment to inventory management through last-mile delivery (Hübner et al., 2016). We offer standard integration of our services to major e-commerce platforms so you can scale your business as big as possible and we will take care how it gets there.
